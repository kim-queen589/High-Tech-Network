Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Profile

How do I change my password?
Go to Settings > Security Settings, enter your current password, and then set your new password.
How can I update my profile information?
Navigate to Settings > Profile Settings to update your personal information and profile picture.

Investments

How do I start investing?
Go to the Invest Now page, choose your preferred investment plan, and follow the instructions to complete your investment.
When do I receive my profits?
Profits are credited to your account daily based on your investment plan. You can view your earnings in the dashboard.

Withdrawals

How do I withdraw my earnings?
Go to the Withdraw page, select your preferred payment method, enter the amount you wish to withdraw, and follow the instructions.
What is the minimum withdrawal amount?
The minimum withdrawal amount is Rs 1,000. This applies to all payment methods.
